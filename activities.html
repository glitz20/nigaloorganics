GHOREPANI TRAIL HIKING

August-October and February-April
Cost:Included in the package
Ghorepani trail is one of the best trail in Nepal. Starting from our resort, you will walk through scenic mountains and hills for roughly 35 miles with total incination of 800m. Ghorepani has a great hill station where you can get best view of mountains including Mt. Dhaulagiri, Mt. Annapurna and Mt. Machhapuchre.

PADDY FESTIVAL

June 20- July 05
Cost:Included in the package
Rice is planted in a unique way in Nepal. Infact this process is celebrated as a festival in Nepal. Its a fun way to learn about rice farming in Nepal. You get to plant the rice by yourself, get involved in dance, music and of course deliuious food. Hundred of guest come to our resort during this time to experience this festival.
