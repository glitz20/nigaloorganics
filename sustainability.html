SUSTAINABILITY

ALL NATURAL

We pride ourselves in producing all natural foods that are consumed in Nigalo Resort and Eateries. We keep ourselves away from GMOsm Pesticides and other chemical fertilizers. At Nigalo, your health and satisfaction is our priamry concern and we aim to provide you all with healthy option. With more than 250 acres of farm land, we have variety of supply ranging from dairy to fruits.

We have numerous options in our dinning. Be assured to go all natural when you are at Nigalo. In addition to dining you can also directly pick and ask the cook to preapre for you. Chances are you will be eating the fish you caught.

GREEN CERTIFICATION

Our resort is three star certified meaning we took care of the environment in the best way possible during the construction period. Our bulding and facilities are eco-friendly and designed to be energy efficient. In addition to that most of the landscape at Nigalo is natural. We let it go as it is and keep it all natural.

Miles away from settlement you can get peace of mind here. We have minimum pollution and all you can have here is clean and fresh air.

EFFICIENT ENERGY

Currently we have 60% of our total energy system powered by Solar energy. This has allowed us to cut on our carbon emission and provide us with clean energy. We are proud to say that we are 100% energy independent. The remaining 40% of energy is provided by our own hydro electricity station. Utilizing the free flow of stream that runs through our lush forest we are able to convert kinetic energy into electrical.

We have many interesting and eco-friendly activities planned. You can learn more about it when you have Nigalo experience. We hope to show our systems and surroundings some day soon.

ALL ORGANIC

Right from the start, all our animals and plants are feeded with organic materials. We believe that organic growth is best of all of us. Although we might need to compromise in time, we cannot compromise in terms of health and quality. As a result our fruits have great taste compared to chemically grown ones. In addition to that our meats and dairy have more quality nutrition.

You can get the oppurtunity to learn more about organic food and healthy lifestyle. It is high time that all of us realize about the importance of organic food as it is for our own good.
